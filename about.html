About

App Icon Studio helps teams generate icon exports faster.

App Icon Studio is a browser-based tool for founders, designers, and developers who need Android and iOS app icon packages without manually resizing every file. The goal is to keep icon preparation simple, visual, and easier to hand off into real app projects.

What the product does

You can upload one source image, preview how it looks with different spacing and shape settings, and export a structured ZIP for common Android and iOS icon requirements.

Who it is for

The workflow is built for small product teams, indie builders, agencies, and mobile app developers who want a faster route from design asset to platform-ready icon package.

Why it exists

Preparing icon sizes by hand is repetitive and easy to get wrong. App Icon Studio keeps previews, sizing, and export packaging in one place so teams can spend less time on setup work and more time shipping.

What you can export

The current public workflow supports Android launcher outputs, Google Play artwork, and iOS AppIcon assets so the same source graphic can be prepared for more than one platform.

How the workflow stays simple

The tool focuses on practical controls such as color, zoom, padding, and shape, plus visual preview surfaces that help you catch spacing or readability issues before download.

Privacy and product trust

App Icon Studio also provides public pages such as the privacy policy and sitemap so visitors, search engines, and review systems can understand what the site offers and how it operates.
