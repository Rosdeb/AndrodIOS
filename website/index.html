About Appicon Studio

Built for teams that want a faster way to move from logo asset to export-ready app icon package.

About The Product

A small icon workflow with a full product mindset

Appicon Studio is designed for founders, designers, and mobile teams who need a practical way to shape an app icon, preview it in context, and export the required Apple and Android assets without building every file by hand.

The studio keeps the workflow simple: upload, refine, preview, and export. The goal is to help you judge the icon as a product surface, not only as a square image.

What You Get
Live Studio Editing

Adjust icon text, colors, shape, zoom, and padding with instant visual feedback.

Device & Store Preview

Review the icon on an iPhone-style screen and in larger promotional surfaces before export.

Clean Export Package

Download Android sizes, iOS app icons, and store graphics with a simpler package structure.
